2. Grouping content

Grouping elements are listed below.

Paragraph

This is a paragraph of text. Some of the text may be emphasised and some it might even be strongly emphasised. Occasionally quoted text may be found within a paragraph …and of course a link may appear at any point in the text. The average paragraph contains five or six sentences although some may contain as little or one or two while others carry on for anything up to ten sentences and beyond.

3. Text-level semantics

Text-level elements are listed below.

Link and anchor

Visit my drinks page.

Stressed emphasis

I must say I adore lemonade.

Importance

This tea is very hot.

Side comments (small print)

These grapes are made into wine. Alcohol is addictive.

Inaccurate text

Price: £4.50 £2.00!

Titles of works (citations)

The case Hugo v. Danielle is relevant here.

Inline quotes

The judge said You can drink water from the fish tank but advised against it.

Definition

The term organic food refers to food produced without synthetic chemicals.

Abbreviation

Organic food in Ireland is certified by the IOFGA.

Machine-readable equivalent of date- or time-related data

Available starting on November 12th!

Computer code

To get started with HTML5 you just need at this top of your page: <!DOCTYPE html>

Variable

If there are n fruit in the bowl, at least n÷2 will be ripe.

Computer output

The computer said Unknown error -3.

User input

Hit F1 to continue.

Subscript text

Water is H2O.

Superscript text

Today is the 2nd of May.

Alternative voice (italicized)

Lemonade consists primarily of Citrus limon.

Emboldened keywords

Take a lemon and squeeze it with a juicer.

Annotations

The mixture of apple juice and eldeflower juice is very pleasant.

Marked or highlighted text

Elderflower cordial, with one part cordial to ten parts water, stands apart from the rest.

Ruby annotations

OJ (Orange Juice)

Text directionality isolation

The recommended restaurant is My Juice Café (At The Beach).

Text directionality formatting

The proposal is to write English, but in reverse order. "Juice" would become "Juice".

4. Edits

The ins and del elements represent edits to the document.

Addition

The ins element represents an addition to the document. I like fruit.

Removal

The del element represents a removal from the document. Download more tracks.
